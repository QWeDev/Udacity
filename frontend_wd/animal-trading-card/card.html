Red Panda

Their ankle joints can rotate, allowing them to climb down trees headfirst—a rare and impressive skill!

  • Scientific Name: Ailurus fulgens
  • Average Length: 38 inches
  • Average Lifespan: 8 - 10 years
  • Habitat: Temperate Forests

Red pandas are playful, tree-dwelling mammals native to the eastern Himalayas and southwestern China. Red pandas are mostly active at dawn and dusk and have a diet that’s surprisingly 95% bamboo, though they also eat fruit, eggs, and insects.
